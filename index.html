    How to order

    You may follow the following steps to successfully place an order and to track your order.

    1.  Select the items you want from our products section by clicking the "add to basket" button
    2.  Check your list and the total price by clicking the basket icon to open and close the basket
    3.  When you are content with your basket contents click the "checkout" button to generate an "order summary". Copy the summary
    4.  Click the "order now" button located in on the home page to open our whatsapp chat. Paste the summary
    5.  When your order has been processed, you will recieve a live location to track it
    6.  Pay upon delivery and dont forget to recommend the site

    Please note that we are currently operating on a pay on delivery basis while we work towards full online ordering and building trust.
